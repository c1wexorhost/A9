Cross Sonic Logging (CSL) Testing Tubes

Cross sonic logging testing tubes, CSL tubes are considered to be more accurate and reliable than sonic echo testing. This testing method is not only able to detect defects, but it can help provide information on the nature of the defect, along with its extent, depth, and approximate location.

Step-1

Install a bottom cap onto the first sonic tube.

Step-2

Fix the sonic tube to the rebar cage by tying the steel wire around the fixing ears and tube.

Step-3

Fix the next tube to the rebar cage as the step 2.

Step-4

Connect the two tubes completely

Step-5

Connect all the tubes and lower the cage.

Final

Fast and easy installation. Push-fit assembly. No welding required at job site. No equipment required. Easy fixing to rebar cage.
